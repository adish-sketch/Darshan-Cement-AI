01 — How it works

Photo in. Repair plan out.

No special equipment. Just a phone camera and something to scale against — an A4 sheet, a coin, or a tape measure already lying in your kit.

STEP 1

Capture

Snap or upload a photo of the hole, crack, or damaged patch that needs filling.

STEP 2

Detect

The vision model finds the void's edges and traces its outline automatically.

STEP 3

Calibrate

Mark a known reference object once — every pixel is now a real measurement.

STEP 4

Estimate

Get length, width, area, volume, and a full material + cost breakdown.
